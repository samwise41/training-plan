## 3. Training Parameters

**Methodology:** 80/20 Science-Based Periodization.

* **80%:** Zone 2 - Builds mitochondrial density.
* **20%:** Zone 4/5 - Raises the performance ceiling.

### Cycling Power Zones (Based on 241W FTP)

* **Zone 1:** < 133W
* **Zone 2:** 134W – 180W
* **Zone 3:** 181W – 216W
* **Zone 4:** 217W – 253W
* **Zone 5:** 254W – 289W

### Running Heart Rate Zones (Based on 171 bpm LTHR)

* **Zone 1:** < 139 bpm
* **Zone 2:** 140 – 152 bpm
* **Zone 3:** 153 – 161 bpm
* **Zone 4:** 162 – 170 bpm
* **Zone 5a:** 171 – 174 bpm
* **Zone 5b:** 175 – 181 bpm
* **Zone 5c:** > 182 bpm
